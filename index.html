Our Collective Declaration

We, the residents of this society, stand united behind a shared vision — forged in transparency, accountability, and the unwavering belief that a community in harmony can achieve the extraordinary. This manifesto is our collective declaration, our binding commitment, and our promise to every resident who calls this place home.

I

Chapter One

Financial & Legal
Accountability

⬛ Highest Priority
01
Financial Recovery

Recovery of Misappropriated Maintenance Funds

A sum exceeding ₹7 Crore in maintenance funds is currently held illegally by the builder. The recovery of these funds is our single most urgent mandate. We will pursue every legal, regulatory, and administrative avenue available until what rightfully belongs to this society is returned — in full, without compromise, and without delay.

02
Legal & Collective Action

Escalating the Fight Against the Builder

The builder's illegal policies and brazen attempts at land encroachment will be met with organised, relentless, and legally sound resistance. We will deepen our alliance with the Vihar Society to present a unified front — coordinating legal strategy, sharing resources, and fighting together until we achieve a decisive and tangible victory for all residents.

II

Chapter Two

Infrastructure &
Structural Improvements

03
Drainage & Flooding

Complete Overhaul of the Society Drainage System

Waterlogging during monsoon is not an inconvenience — it is a systemic failure we will not tolerate. We commit to a full structural redesign of the drainage network, engineered to eliminate flooding at its root. Simultaneously, the Nahar repair effort — now elevated with an on-site Senior Government Officer visit — marks the start of a sustained, government-backed campaign to resolve seepage permanently.

04
Structural & Aesthetic

Painting of Towers & Professional Seepage Treatment

The external appearance and structural integrity of our towers are a direct reflection of our standards. We will initiate a comprehensive painting programme for all towers, paired with professional, expert-led treatment of all seepage-affected areas. This will not be a cosmetic exercise — it will be a thorough, lasting intervention.

05
Expert-Led Works

Structural Oversight by a Dedicated Expert Team

All major structural concerns — including the management and maintenance of the Sewage Treatment Plant (STP) and Water Treatment Plant (WTP) — will be placed under the supervision of a qualified panel of professional experts. This ensures critical civil and mechanical infrastructure is never left to chance or compromise.

06
Lifts & Safety

Revamp of Lift Infrastructure & Fire Safety Systems

Safety is non-negotiable. We will draft and execute a new, comprehensive contract with KONE covering lift maintenance, mandatory safety audits, and updated service-level commitments. This will be accompanied by the installation of modern fire alarm systems across all towers — because no resident should ever question the safety of their building.

07
Water Supply

Revamp of Water Supply Infrastructure

Reliable, clean water is a fundamental right of every resident. We will undertake a complete overhaul of the society's water supply network, including the procurement of a new, high-capacity water pump, to ensure consistent and uninterrupted supply to all towers.

08
Housekeeping

Reimagined Housekeeping & Gardening Services

The cleanliness and greenery of our common spaces are the baseline of dignified communal living. We will overhaul housekeeping and gardening services — instituting clear standards, accountability measures, and regular audits that ensure our society is always at its best.

09
Sustainability

Solar Panel Installation on Rooftops

The future is renewable, and this society will be part of it. We will drive the installation of solar panels across rooftop spaces — reducing electricity costs for residents, lowering our collective carbon footprint, and demonstrating that a community of this calibre takes its responsibilities to the future seriously.

III

Chapter Three

Community, Wellbeing
& Belonging

10
Children & Youth

Investing in the Youngest Members of Our Society

A society is only as vibrant as its children. A child who grows up with space to run, play, and belong grows into someone who cares for the world around them. We commit to:

  • Upgradation and active maintenance of existing playground facilities
  • Development of new recreational infrastructure — volleyball net, basketball hoop, and dedicated play zones
  • Society-funded sports tournaments to foster team spirit and pride — because a society that plays together, stays together

11
Senior Residents

A Dedicated Helpline & Emergency Support for Senior Residents

Our elders built the fabric of this community. It is our duty to ensure they are never left without support. We will establish a dedicated helpline for all senior residents — where a single call or the press of a button dispatches a trained attendant directly to their home to address any emergency, concern, or need. No senior in this society should ever feel alone or unheard.

IV

Chapter Four

Governance &
Digital Transparency

⬛ Highest Priority
12
Digital Platform

Society Website & Open Community Forum

Transparency is not possible without open channels of communication. We will build and launch a comprehensive digital platform serving as the central hub for all society matters — featuring a structured portal for complaints, suggestions, and critique, where every resident's voice is documented, acknowledged, and acted upon. No more closed-door decisions.

13
Governance

Increased Delegation & Decentralisation of Decision-Making

Decisions that affect every resident should not rest in the hands of a select few. We will restructure our governance model to devolve meaningful decision-making authority to the tower level — each tower empowered with its own elected representatives, its own voice, and genuine autonomy over matters that directly affect its residents. A society governed closest to the people it serves is a society that truly works.
